About.

Welcome! We're all about making meal times more meaningful. For us, it's not just about the food, but about being mindful, appreciating culture, and caring for each other.

We're here to celebrate the simple act of eating together.

Think about it: around the world, tables are where families share stories, discuss ideas, and grow closer. Our goal is to help you make these moments more thoughtful, more sustainable, and more fun, one plate at a time.

We want to help you find the joy in everyday meals again.

🌿 Our Story

It started when we realized that life gets so busy, meals can become rushed and impersonal. But how we eat – the dishes we select, the care we put in, the attention we give – really has an effect on how we feel, how we bond with others, and how we live our lives.

Even something as basic as your everyday plate has a big impact. It's the backdrop for nourishment, balance, and beauty to come together. What we eat, how we serve it, and our values, all show in this one spot.

That's how it became our goal to rethink the basic plate completely. We desired to share ideas and designs that inspire everyone to eat with awareness, live sustainably, and notice the pleasure in everyday rituals.

As time went on, we started thinking about more than just dishes. It turned into a way of life. We wanted to stop and slow down, keep things simple, and focus on what really matters.

🍽️ Our Mission

Our goal is to make dining better with designs that are thoughtful, materials that are good for the planet, and respect for old ideas and the desire to try out new things.

Everyone should use plates—and everything else where what is important everyday—showing care, being useful, and having meaning behind it, specially the one which serve as the background of the most important eating experience of the day.

We have three main focuses:

Conscious eating: to help people enjoy good meals, understand how food, health, and design all connect.

Sustainability: to promote materials that are good for the earth and encourage everyone to be aware on what they eat to help protect our planet.

The art of daily living: to bring beauty, personal ties and mindfulness to even the smallest moments in life.

⚙️ What We Believe

1. Simplicity

We like things easy, useful, and pretty. The best designs do what they're meant to do without being flashy. Being simple means the single-serve plate or a multi-compartment works will always be useful.

2. Durability

Things should be good to resist through use and time and long lasting. A plate should be good to resist through countless celebrations, experiences, and meals around it.

3. Sustainability

We are always aware of protecting our earth, and it shows through all. From choosing reusable ceramic or steel plates to looking at plant-based other options, we are sure that small every day choices can create a real and good impact in our world.

4. Mindfulness

Your plate designs, amount of food, and time to eat, influence your health. A mindful plate set helps routine meal become a nourishing time for both body and mind.

🥗 Our View: The Plate as a Blank Space

Plates are a blank canvas—a space for imagination, balance, and beauty.

The canvas teaches us about portion, harmony of textures and colors present in the food we eat.

We suggest the ½–¼–¼ method for meals that are balanced:

Fill half the plate with vegetables and fruits

A quarter of the plate filled with grains

The other left quarter of the plate filled with healthy portions of protein

This isnt only about health, is also about making you feel happy. A plate that is complete feels comforting visually and satisfying.

🌍 The World View

Food makes us be together regardless of where you are. Each culture has its own way of plating food. There is the round steel of India, the lunchbox like style of the bento trays of Japan, the common ceramic platters of the Mediterranean and the plates form the Western part of the world.

We love these different ways of plating. Each design is about values: about helping each other, having balance, sharing, and being aware. With time we want to learn and fuse modern needs by merging old ideas and adding innovation to it.

🧑‍🍳 Our Main Point: Plates

We're all about plates. They are not just product to us, we view it more of a tool to assist in having a healthy life.

We see:

The many materials can be used in making Plates. Ceramic, Glass, Steel, Melamine, Palm Leaf, Bamboo and more options.

The bad and the good of each option depending on the usage, lifestyle and overall environment.

The right dimensions and shapes for the serving variations and different meal types.

Tips for long-term cleanliness, protection and routine maintenance.

Guidelines in finding alternative options that are safe for earth and having proper disposal methods.

Inspiration from eating practices rooted in different societies.

💡 Our Vision

🧵 Our Promise

We do not push sales or promote any products. We help to educate, so that you pick the right choice for your house, your values and to your family.

A long-term purpose is to mix what we know, make innovative product, with earth-friendly products to let people feel like everyone create conscious ways to approach dining.

🫱 Community
